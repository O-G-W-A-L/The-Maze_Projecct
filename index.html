About

In the heart of my coding journey, I stood at a crossroads. Websites and apps had been my companions, but the siren song of game design beckoned—a maze of pixels and possibilities and purpose. A time in my life that i felt someone should play what i desgined. Week one was a harsh. Raycasting, that mathematical storm, swept me off my feet. I grappled with vectors, angles, and pixel shaders. Frustration brewed like a storm, yet I clung to the mast of curiosity. Then came week two—a sunburst after rain. My foot pressed the pedal, and the maze unfolded. Walls rose, corridors snaked, and the minotaur of doubt prowled. But I danced with algorithms, each step a revelation, a triumph over initial chaos. This portfolio project for Holberton School allowed me to combine my passion for mazes with my skills in game development.
